### [1] [Contrarian Majority Dynamics: Violation of Detailed Balance and Nonequilibrium Steady States](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.02358)
*Serge Galam*

Main category: cond-mat.stat-mech

TL;DR: 本文从统计力学角度重新审视了带有反对者的Galam多数模型（GMM），揭示了其动力学本质：GMM并非传统意义上的平均场近似，而是一种迭代的平均场动力学；单个智能体与同步更新机制虽产生相同演化方程，但前者满足Kolmogorov环条件，后者则破坏细致平衡且存在非零概率流，表明其处于真实的非平衡定常态。

Motivation: 澄清Galam多数模型（GMM）在统计力学框架下的基础问题，特别是其演化方程是否为平均场近似的结果，并探究其非平衡特性。

Method: 构建单智能体随机动力学以对应原GMM的同步更新机制，推导传统多数规则相互作用的平均场动力学并与GMM对比，分析两种更新方式下系统对细致平衡和Kolmogorov环条件的满足情况，并计算定常态下的概率流。

Result: 发现GMM不是标准的平均场近似，而是迭代的平均场动力学；单智能体更新满足Kolmogorov环条件但违反细致平衡，而同步更新同时违反两者；定常态下存在非零概率流，证明系统处于非平衡定常态。

Conclusion: Galam多数模型本质上是一个非平衡过程，其稳态无有效哈密顿量描述，反对者不等同于热噪声，模型表现出不同层次的不可逆性。

Abstract: I revisit the Galam Majority Model (GMM) with contrarian agents from a statistical-mechanics perspective, revealing three fundamental features. First, in addition to the GMM simultaneous-update of small discussion groups, I construct a related single-agent stochastic dynamics, providing a Markovian microscopic representation, which is found to yield the same evolution equation. Second, I show that, contrary to what is often stated in the literature, the GMM closed evolution equation for the opinion density is not the result of a mean-field approximation. Indeed, I derive the conventional mean-field dynamics associated with majority-rule interactions and show that it yields a distinct, probabilistic evolution equation contrary the deterministic GMM equation. I therefore identify the GMM as an iterated mean-field dynamics. Third, I investigate the thermodynamic nature of the dynamics obtained from both single-agent and simultaneous updates. Both are shown to violate detailed balance. However, while Kolmogorov's cycle condition is satisfied for single-agent updates, it is violated for simultaneous updates, making the departure from equilibrium stronger in the latter case. I then compute the probability flux in the stationary state and show that it is non-vanishing, confirming the absence of an effective Hamiltonian and establishing that the stationary state is a genuine nonequilibrium steady state.These results clarify the statistical-mechanical foundations of the GMM and establish contrarian majority dynamics as an intrinsically non-equilibrium process with distinct regimes of irreversibility. Contrarians are not thermal noise.
