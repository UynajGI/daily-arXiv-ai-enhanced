Main category: nlin.CD

TL;DR: 提出了一种新的线性化动力学方法，修正了广义刘维尔方程，以保持相空间体积不变，适用于非哈密顿系统，并通过稳定性矩阵的反对称部分定义时间演化算符，实现了正交变换而不扭曲体积元。

Motivation: 传统的切空间动力学会导致相空间体积在混沌系统中消失，因此需要一种能够保持相空间体积不变的新方法，特别是在非哈密顿系统中。

Method: 基于经典密度矩阵理论，利用稳定性矩阵的反对称部分定义时间演化算符，生成不改变体积的正交变换；同时用对称部分构造非正交算符来描述体积元的可压缩性。

Result: 实现了对耗散、瞬态或受驱混沌系统的切空间动力学基矢量的完整分析，无需重新正交化；计算了瞬时李雅普诺夫指数谱和局部吉布斯熵流率，并在多个经典系统中验证了数值上的便利性。

Conclusion: 该方法为非哈密顿系统的相空间体积守恒提供了有效的理论框架，给出了类似量子力学的刘维尔-冯·诺依曼方程的演化方程，具有广泛的应用前景。

Abstract: Infinitesimal volumes stretch and contract as they coevolve with classical
phase space trajectories according to a linearized dynamics. Unless these
tangent space dynamics are modified, the underlying chaotic dynamics will cause
the volume to vanish as tangent vectors collapse on the most expanding
direction. Here, we propose an alternative linearized dynamics and rectify the
generalized Liouville equation to preserve phase space volume, even for
non-Hamiltonian systems. Within a classical density matrix theory, we define
the time evolution operator from the anti-symmetric part of the stability
matrix so that phase space volume is time-invariant. The operator generates
orthogonal transformations without distorting volume elements, providing an
invariant measure for dissipative dynamics and a evolution equation for the
density matrix akin to the quantum mechanical Liouville-von Neumann equation.
The compressibility of volume elements is determined by a non-orthogonal
operator made from the symmetric part of the stability matrix. We analyze
complete sets of basis vectors for the tangent space dynamics of chaotic
systems, which may be dissipative, transient or driven, without
re-orthogonalization of tangent vectors. The linear harmonic oscillator, the
Lorenz-Fetter model, and the H\'enon-Heiles system demonstrate the computation
of the instantaneous Lyapunov exponent spectrum and the local Gibbs entropy
flow rate using these bases and show that it is numerically convenient.

### [70] [Exactly solvable Stuart-Landau models in arbitrary dimensions](https://arxiv.org/abs/2511.05160)
*Pragjyotish Bhuyan Gogoi,Rahul Ghosh,Debashis Ghoshal,Awadhesh Prasad,Ram Ramaswamy*

Main category: nlin.CD

TL;DR: 本文利用Clifford几何代数将Stuart-Landau系统推广到高于二维的情况，并在一般情况下给出了振子方程的精确解。在超临界Hopf分岔处，系统表现出极端多稳性，即无限多个具有环面几何结构的极限轨道共存，运动表现为周期或准周期。

  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 将Stuart-Landau系统推广到高维空间，研究其在超临界Hopf分岔下的动力学行为，特别是多稳性和高维振荡结构。

Method: 采用Clifford几何代数方法对Stuart-Landau系统进行高维扩展，并分析其在固定点处的雅可比矩阵特征值行为，研究振荡动力学在高维超球面上的演化特性。

Result: 在超临界Hopf分岔时，系统存在N对共轭复特征值同时穿越虚轴（奇数维时还有一个实特征值），振荡被限制在D-1维超球面上，出现无限多个共存的环面状极限轨道，表现为周期或准周期运动。

Conclusion: 通过Clifford代数方法成功实现了Stuart-Landau系统的高维推广，并揭示了其在高维情况下的极端多稳性和复杂振荡结构，为其他极限环系统的高维扩展提供了思路。

Abstract: We use Clifford's geometric algebra to extend the Stuart-Landau system to
dimensions $D >2$ and give an exact solution of the oscillator equations in the
general case. At the supercritical Hopf bifurcation marked by a transition from
stable fixed-point dynamics to oscillatory motion, the Jacobian matrix
evaluated at the fixed point has $N=\lfloor{D/2}\rfloor$ pairs of complex
conjugate eigenvalues which cross the imaginary axis simultaneously. For odd
$D$ there is an additional purely real eigenvalue that does the same.
Oscillatory dynamics is asymptotically confined to a hypersphere
$\mathbb{S}^{D-1}$ and is characterised by extreme multistability, namely the
coexistence of an infinite number of limiting orbits each of which has the
geometry of a torus $\mathbb{T}^N$ on which the motion is either periodic or
quasiperiodic. We also comment on similar Clifford extensions of other limit
cycle oscillator systems and their generalisations.
