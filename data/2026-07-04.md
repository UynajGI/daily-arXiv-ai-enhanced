### [1] [Analysis of the asymptotic density of endogamous diploid cellular automata](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.01917)
*Henryk Fuk{ś},Nazim Fatès*

Main category: nlin.CG

TL;DR: 研究了二倍体初等细胞自动机（ECA）的渐近行为，即两种ECA之间的随机混合。根据密度随混合程度λ变化的曲线特征，提出了六类分类，并分析了局部结构近似在预测渐近密度方面的有效性。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 探讨在具有反射或共轭对称性的二倍体ECA中，规则混合比例λ如何影响系统长期行为（特别是密度演化），并评估理论近似方法（局部结构法）的预测能力。

Method: 通过数值模拟分析不同λ值下二倍体ECA的密度演化，依据密度-λ曲线形状进行六类划分；应用局部结构近似理论在不同阶数下计算并比较渐近密度。

Result: 发现当密度与λ呈线性关系时，局部结构近似能精确预测；非线性但可导情况下，高阶近似快速收敛；一阶相变可通过高阶近似逼近，但二阶相变即使高阶近似也无法捕捉其特征；此外，成功计算出两个二倍体规则在每一步的精确密度。

Conclusion: 局部结构近似在多数情况下有效，尤其适用于线性和部分非线性系统，但在处理二阶相变时存在根本局限，揭示了该方法在复杂相变行为建模中的不足。

Abstract: We investigate the asymptotic behaviour of diploid Elementary Cellular Automata (ECA), that is, the stochastic mixtures between two ECAs. In this model, each cell independently applies one rule with probability $λ$ and the other rule with probability $ 1 - λ$. Focusing on the endogamous diploids where the two ECAs are related by the reflection or conjugation symmetry, we analyse how the density varies as function of $λ$, the ``degree of mixing'' of these two rules. We propose a classification into six distinct classes depending on the profile of the density vs. $λ$ curve. We take various examples for each class and we analyse to which extent the local structure approximation succeeds to predict the asymptotic density. Our results show that for rules in which the asymptotic density depends linearly on $λ$, the local structure approximation reproduces the exact dependence of the density on $λ$. For rules with differentiable but nonlinear dependence, the approximation either becomes exact at a finite order or converges rapidly to the exact solution as the order increases. For rules with first-order phase transitions, finite-order approximations progressively approach a sharp transition profile with increasing order. In contrast, for rules exhibiting a second-order phase transition, even high-order approximations fail to capture the qualitative features of the transition. (no bifurcation is observed). Finally, we give examples of two diploid rules for which we succeed to compute the density at each time step.

### [2] [GPU-accelerated superiorization on constrained physical problems with SupPy](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.27086)
*Tobias Becher,Yair Censor,Kay Barshad,Niklas Wahl*

Main category: physics.comp-ph

TL;DR: 本研究将优越化方法（SM）应用于地震成像重建、低剂量CT重建和调强放射治疗计划三个应用物理问题，并开发了开源Python工具包SupPy，结果表明优越化算法在减少噪声和降低辐射剂量方面优于单纯可行性搜索算法。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 优越化方法介于可行性搜索与约束优化之间，已有研究受限于缺乏公开可用的软件工具，本文旨在通过开发开源工具SupPy推动该方法在更多领域的应用。

Method: 使用自主开发的模块化Python工具包SupPy，在CPU和GPU上实现可行性搜索算法及其优越化版本，并将其应用于三个物理应用问题：地震图像重建、低剂量CT重建和调强放疗计划设计。

Result: 在三个应用中，优越化算法均优于单纯的可行性搜索算法：成像任务中噪声更低，放疗计划中身体受照剂量更低；特别是在放疗案例中，优越化方法能在不可行约束条件下生成临床上可行的治疗方案。

Conclusion: 优越化方法在实际物理应用中具有显著优势，结合开源工具SupPy可促进其在更广泛科研领域中的采用与验证。

Abstract: The superiorization method (SM) is situated between feasibility-seeking and constrained optimization. Instead of aiming at the minimum of a given objective function over a constraint set, it seeks a feasible point at which the objective function value is reduced - though not necessarily minimal - rather than hard targets, or in which a mathematically optimal solution is not strictly required. While the method has been investigated for several applications in physics, its broader use has been limited, in part due to the lack of openly available software for researchers wishing to explore it.
  In this work we apply superiorization to three problems from applied physics: seismic image reconstruction, low-dose CT reconstruction and intensity-modulated radiotherapy treatment planning. These experiments are conducted with SupPy, an open-source modularized Python toolbox developed for this work, which supports execution of feasibility-seeking algorithms and their superiorized version on both the CPU and the GPU. In all three cases the superiorized algorithms achieve favorable results compared to feasibility-seeking alone, with reduced noise in the imaging examples and lowered body dose in the radiotherapy plans. For the radiotherapy case we further observe that superiorization produces clinically viable plans on infeasible constraint sets.
